Roadmap — where the whole system is

Three tracks of work, one view. Build order was dependency-driven: connect → make reliable → make intelligent → make autonomous. Current tracked items are shipped, upstream-owned, accepted non-goals, or trigger-gated by required evidence volume.

The thesis: shipped work so far is the nervous system — distinct content per account, quality-gated, reliably scheduled, with feedback that returns. "Smart" arrives at F (learning loop) and G (autonomy), sequenced last on purpose — they can't learn until the lower layers produce real, complete cycles. AP0-2 (reliable feedback) + A–D (real per-account variation to measure) are F's preconditions, now in place.
