The content lifecycle, as one system

The map that didn't exist before: content's full journey across both repos. Forward — creator-os builds draft payloads and HTTP-POSTs them (secret-authed) to a TD ingest endpoint that lands them in the posts table; a legacy direct-Supabase-write path is present but gated off. Backward — TD accumulates per-post metrics in post_metric_history; creator-os pulls them (manually) into its learning loop. The loop is architecturally closed but operationally manual on the return leg, and the forward leg's idempotency/media guarantees are softer than they look. Full detail in INFRA_LIFECYCLE_AUDIT.md; fix plan in INFRA_HARDENING_PROMPT.md.
